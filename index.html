About

Proverbs 22:6 - Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it.

With God's intention, plan and direction, Nj's Christian School started as a day care in March, 2008 (Kahawa West).

The school has adopted an International Christian curriculum known as A.C.E (Accelerated Christian Education), hence the name, Nj's Christian School. The curriculum empowers our students by nurturing their academic, social, physical and most importantly, their spiritual development.

Our responsibility is to instill values that shape the child's character with a disciplined mindset that can be seen in and out of school.

Why Nj's Kindergarten

Intellect, principles and talents should be nurtured in a child from an early age and that is what Nj's Christian School is about.

Why Nj's Primary

We have taken the initiative of implementing e-learning so the child does not miss out on learning even if they are away from school. The child is able to catch up through the assignments in the student portal.

Co-curricular Activities

We believe that learning extra skills are needed so as to empower every boy and girl to be the best they can be.

Abacus

Abacus is a method to achieve speed and accuracy of calculations. Children develop the following mental abilities : concentration, observation, vlsualization & imagination, and memory.

Computer

Technology has become a fundamental skill that is needed by everyone. We are here to equip the child with the necessary skills needed, and by this we offer computer classes.

Music

Music is a way of learning. Not only does one get to acquire a new skill but for a child it also helps in; increasing coordination, concentration and enhance self-esteem.

Swimming

All work without play makes Jack a dull boy. We believe that the child should learn swimming skills because its healthy and it gives them a chance to bond with their fellow students.

Trips

Learning goes beyond the classroom. Educational trips help the child learn more and discover skills that go beyond the classroom. This can also help them in making career choices in the future.

Foreign Languages

We offer Chinese , Spanish, German and French to holistically expose the child to different cultural enrichment. It also gives them a chance to achieve higher levels academically.
